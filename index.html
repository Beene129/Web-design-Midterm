Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, and Proximity

Below, I examine four websites, covering the four essential web design principles, as titled above.

Contrast

Apple
  • Apple web site showed a great example of “Contrast” by having a black background.
  • The black and silver camera floating around in the center of the page.
  • The clickable links which bring up more to the menu as you hover over them, they get brighter.

Repetition

Amazon
  1. Amazon has shown great repetition on their website by the way placed their items in different categories with white backgrounds behind each one and by having different layers.
  2. And even the % off was used flush left on each item.
  3. The footer categories were set it repetition as well how they highlighted.

Alignment

Medium
Why Just the Shop Page?

Proximity

Airbnb

  3. This website gives you a map the proximity on the time that you will be spending at the location.
